## Returns

<Spacing size="sm" />

<Expandable title={"Our returns policy"}>
If you change your mind, we're happy to accept returns or exchange your item within 14 days once you receive your package. Please note that we do not accept returns for earrings due to hygiene reasons. We are also unable to accept returns for engraved or made to order items as these are made specially for you. Shipping charges, duties and taxes are non-refundable.
<Spacing size="sm" />
The items must be returned unworn and in their original packaging. We aim to process the refund within 14 working days once it has been received and inspected by us, provided that the items reach us in their original condition. All refunds will be credited to the original payment method.
<Spacing size="sm" />
We offer a standard one-year warranty on all of our jewellery, in which we are happy to exchange or refund the items for any manufacturing defects.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title={"How to return my order?"}>
All orders can be returned within 14 days of receiving your order. Please follow the instructions below:
<Spacing size="sm" />
1. Go to clairlondon.co.uk/create-return. Enter your order and email address to retrieve your order details.
<Spacing size="sm" />
2. Select the item(s) that you would like to return.
<Spacing size="sm" />
3. Once you have submitted the return request, you will receive a confirmation email from us with the return postal address.
<Spacing size="sm" />
4. Send your parcel back to us with a tracked postal service.
<Spacing size="sm" />
Please note that we currently do not offer free returns and the return cost will be borne by you, but we are working on it.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title={"Can I still get a refund for an order with a free gift?"}>
If you received a free gift with your order, you will have to return the free gift if you are no longer eligible for the free gift.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title={"Can I get a refund for sale items?"}>
If you received a tiered discount as part of a multi-buy or minimum spend promotion, the discount may be taken off if your order no longer meets the tiered discount criteria.
</Expandable>
